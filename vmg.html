Vision

To transform the campus into a Women’s University with autonomous operation, refinement in educational quality, availability of resources, innovation in teaching and research, and national and global outreach.


Mission

To run the campus autonomously in order to impart quality education for women to prepare and empower them with knowledge and skills for their life, work and occupations in a distinctive and inclusive learning environment.


Objectives/Goals

The objectives/goals of the institution are:
  • To strengthen academic expertise of the campus by upgrading the faculties with curriculum, research and development of IT-friendly infrastructure;
  • To provide quality education for women empowerment to face the multifarious challenges in practical life in an ever-increasingly complex and competitive world.
  • To provide a congenial learning environment that can fully flourish their potentiality with the scope for the overall development of the student’s personality.
