Bachelor in Computer Application

Bachelor of Computer Applications is a 4-year undergraduate program. It is extended over eight semesters. This program aids the students to get a sound understanding on computer applications which can give them an ideal beginning for a challenging profession in the field of Information Technology. This program prepares students with the necessary skills to build successful careers in the information sector. The educational plan is really tuned for the all encompassing improvement of the students and aims at nurturing them as capable developers and computer professionals. The course curriculum of BCA is designed keeping in view the academic as well as industry requirements of the students. After successful completion of the course, one will have complete information on the best way to use computer' applications to satisfy the client's need utilizing different programming dialects like C, Python, Java and many more. With sound academic knowledge of Computer Applications, one can easily get a job in large IT companies as Software Engineers at various positions at Multi National Companies, IT Industries.


Objectives of Bachelor in Computer Application

The overall objective of BCA program is to impart practical knowledge to solve computer based problems of different types in infrastructure development, social science as well as business and industries for booming development sector worldwide as well as within the nation.

The specific objectives of Bachelor of Arts in Computer Application at the faculty of Humanities and Social Science at Tribhuvan University is :
  • to produce high quality computer application user and developer
  • to produce professionals in the field of computer application as a programmer and software developer
  • to provide knowledge about various tools and techniques used in software development
  • to provide students with both practical and theoretical aspects of studies related to computer application
  • to enhance skills of students to create solutions of technical problem that arise in industrial, commercial as well as in government companies.

    • Eligibility

    Admission Requirements:

    Applicants from any stream ( Science, Management, Humanities and Education) are eligible to apply. However, the students must meet the following criteria for admission:

  • Applicants should have minimum D+ grade in 10+2 .
  • Applicants should pass the entrance examination conducted by TUFOHSS
    • Scholarship:
  • Scholarship will be provided to the students(about 10% of total seats) who have completed her SEE and 10+2 from government institution
  • Semester Toppers, Second Toppers securing at least 3.5 GPA in the final examination are also awarded with partial scholarship.

    • Mission

    It aims at producing skilled technical workforce from the FOHSS.
