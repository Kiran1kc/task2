A Glimpse of Four Year B.Sc. Program in Padma Kanya Multiple Campus

Padmakanya Multiple Campus, the only constituent girls’ campus under Tribhuvan University in Nepal, had first launched Proficiency Certificate Level in Science as one of its main streams in 2026 BS with a view to meet an ever increasing demand for the qualified women graduates in different disciplines of Science and Technology. Ever since then it has been delivering quality education to the girl students producing more efficient, skilled and competent human resources that are able to meet the global challenges as well as a new breed of responsible and upright citizens with moral values. In 2058 BS, the campus introduced a three year bachelor program in Environment Science through the support of Canadian Co-operation Office (CCO) and then B.Sc. Microbiology programme in 2066 BS with a view to produce qualified women graduates in the specialized areas as per needs of the society and country. Since 2069/2070 academic calendar, the campus has been offering a four year B.Sc. programme with specialization in Environment Science, Microbiology and General Science subjects. Located in the very heart of Kathmandu city, PK campus is recognized as an exclusive center of B.Sc. programme among the generations of girl students and parents as being the heart-in-family institute offering science education in making a difference.


Since the introduction of B.Sc. programme, the campus has been fully dedicated implementing modern teaching-learning techniques that are able to enhance students achieving their academic goals. Besides, it has also made tremendous efforts in conducting various indoor as well as outdoor programmes for students, offering adequate opportunities for their active involvement in extra-curricular activities. Within the stature, the campus is operated primarily to fostering students’ positive thinking and a culture of creating knowledge for individuals to be able to live scientifically valued and respectable intellectual and social life. The campus promises learning environment and faculties where young minds being trained for learning in self-motivation, immense individually as well as in team structures in academic, explorative and research practices. These activities help them updated with the recent development issues in various areas of their academic interest through exchange of knowledge and experience with our highly dedicated, skilled and experienced faculty members, subject experts and resource persons. Through such efforts, the campus has been successful maintaining an excellent academic environment as well as good academic track records in science. Over the years, the campus has built up a reputation and position of its own as one of the most sought-after science campuses in Kathmandu valley.
